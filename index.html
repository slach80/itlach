Isaac Lach

Studying under Eva Peng & Dr. Anthony Olson · Class of 2031

Age 12 Walden Middle School GPA 4.0 NJHS Bellus Music Studio Classical Piano

Isaac is a 12-year-old classical pianist from Kansas City, MO whose passion for music has been evident from an early age. He began studying piano at 7 under Mrs. Eva Peng, quickly developing skill and confidence at the keyboard. Over the years his journey has led him to regional, state, and international competitions — earning multiple awards that reflect both his talent and commitment to growth. Known for his natural musicality, Isaac explores a wide range of piano styles, continually expanding his artistic expression. Looking ahead, he hopes to not only perform but also compose his own pieces — bringing his unique voice to life through music.
